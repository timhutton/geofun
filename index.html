Welcome to our playground for geolocation-based fun.

Games with very low privacy concerns:

Tiles Draw all over the map for all to see, in pixels that are 200m across. But you can only paint the tile you are currently in. Privacy: When you paint a tile, we send the tile location and the color to the server. Nothing identifiable is logged.

Games where you should be aware of the privacy issues:

Find your friends! The map shows your location and your friends' locations, in realtime. Go and meetup, or play hide and seek. Privacy: While the page is open you are sharing a location and a username. The location and username are publicly visible for five minutes, then deleted.
